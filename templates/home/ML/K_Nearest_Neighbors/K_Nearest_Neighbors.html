K Nearest Neighbors (Regression & Classification Algorithm)



K Nearest Neighbors

K Nearest Neighbors is a Regression & Classification Algorithm. When used for Regression it's called K Nearest Neighbors Regressor & when it's used for Classification it's called K Nearest Neighbors Classifier

We proceed with assuming a value of k. Let's say 3.

As we can see in the plot picture we have 2 classes - marked as blue(Class 1) & marked as orange(Class 0) and the datapoints are marked in the plots.

The green datapoint marks a new unknown datapoint. So we will consider k nearest known datapoints i.e 3 nearest in this case. We calculate the distance of new unknown datapoint from k nearest known datapoints using formula of Eucliean Distance Thus on the basis of these we will label the new unkown datapoint.
